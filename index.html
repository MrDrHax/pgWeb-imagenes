Where is my privacy?

Made by: Alejandro Fernandez del Valle Herrera
A01024998

For: class AV1002B.701

This collection was inspired by the growing lack of privacy, the immoral ways companies take all our personal info, and the way you are always being watched though modern commodities.

A part of my career path, I am currently researching a lot of these technologies used in our daily devices. All of the information that is available to the public everyday keeps growing and growing, making everything feel unreal, date of birth, name, family, daily routines, health, finances, what we think, what we like, and who we like is part of info collection, a "normal" day in everyone's life. The worst part, is we do not know who this info is taken to, it can be leaked, sold or even just displayed on public. We de not know what the info is being used for, or why is it being taken away in the first place.

The point of this project is to show the world in a simple way some of the things that are taken away from us in a daily basis, how they desguise themselves, and I am trying to make everything intimidate you as the viewer. To pour a little light to what isn't seen everyday.
